## Installation

1. Download the latest APK from the [Releases](https://github.com/YourUsername/App-release/releases) section
2. Enable "Install from Unknown Sources" in your device settings
3. Open the downloaded APK and follow the installation prompts
4. Launch ScreenScape and enjoy your content

## Recommended Providers

For the best experience, we recommend these providers:

- **Netflix Mirror**: Best for latest movies and TV shows
- **MoviesDrive**: Excellent for high-quality downloads
- **MultiStream**: Great alternative for diverse content
- **ProtonMovies**: Reliable for both streaming and downloads
- **FlixHQ**: Good for English content with multiple sources

Different providers work better for different types of content and regions. Experiment to find what works best for you.

## Usage Tips

### Downloading Content
1. Navigate to the content you want to download
2. Select your preferred quality
3. Tap the download button and wait for completion
4. Access your downloads from the Downloads section

### External Player Support
1. Enable "External Player" mode in the content details page
2. Select your preferred server
3. Choose your external player when prompted

### Subtitle Management
1. During playback, tap the screen to show controls
2. Tap the subtitle icon
3. Select your preferred subtitle or add external subtitles

### Casting to TV
1. Ensure your TV and device are on the same network
2. Tap the cast icon during playback
3. Select your casting device
4. Control playback from your mobile device

## FAQ

**Q: Is ScreenScape free to use?**  
A: Yes, ScreenScape is completely free to use with no hidden fees or subscriptions.

**Q: Does ScreenScape require an account?**  
A: No, ScreenScape doesn't require any account creation or login.

**Q: Why does a provider sometimes not work?**  
A: Content providers may sometimes be temporarily unavailable. Try switching to an alternative provider or check again later.

**Q: Can I request new features?**  
A: Yes! Please submit feature requests through the GitHub issues section.

**Q: How do I report bugs?**  
A: Submit any bugs or issues through GitHub issues with detailed descriptions of the problem.

**Q: Is my data private?**  
A: ScreenScape doesn't collect personal data. All viewing history and preferences are stored locally on your device.
